Exploratory Data Analysis of Video Game Sales

Problem Statement
There is a lot of historical data about the sales of video games available on multiple enterprise data sources, including cloud platforms and open sources. These data sources contain a lot of information about the sales of video games, including, but not restricted to, video game platforms, genre, year of launch, sales in different regions, etc. The motive is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA) on one such open source data set. EDA is a method to analyze data to summarize important variables, find trends, and use visualization to understand better the insights that the data can provide. The purpose of this research is to understand the data and its distribution. We will then conclude with any insights drawn from the data. This would be helpful in future decision making.

Real World Impact Exploratory Data Analysis of video game sales, or any other sales for that matter, can help us make impactful business strategies, understand consumer behaviour and as a result, lead to successful business decisions.

  1. Genres: It can help us identify popular genres of games. Decisions can be made about how much investment should be made in certain products once we understand the sales. This can also lead to innovation.
  2. Platform: Based on which platform generates how much sales, a business can decide how to diversify itself.
  3. The trend over the years: The popularity of a certain product can be analyzed by sales done over the years. This can help decide how to increase or decrease investment in them.
  4. Region of sales: Different geographies have different purchase behaviours. It is always preferable to understand the demographics of any product’s sales.
Such key-markers about a product, or a competitor’s product can be studied. EDA helps in multiple fields of a business, from marketing, innovation, sales, technology and budgeting.
